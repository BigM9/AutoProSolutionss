About AutoProSolutions

AutoProSolutions is committed to providing top-tier automotive services. Founded on principles of quality and integrity, we offer a complete range of vehicle solutions, including diagnosis, repairs, bodywork, painting, pre-purchase inspections, and vehicle sales. Our mission is to ensure your vehicle is in optimal condition, whether you're buying, selling, or servicing.

Our MD/CEO, Asiku Martins, is a graduate of Mechanical Engineering from the prestigious Olabisi Onabanjo University. With years of expertise in automotive engineering and a deep passion for customer satisfaction, Asiku leads a team of skilled professionals dedicated to providing the best solutions tailored to each customer's needs. We take pride in our commitment to excellence and transparency in every service we offer.
